// How It Works

Three steps.
That's it.

01

Text the job

Open WhatsApp and describe the job in plain English. Customer name, what you did, parts, hours. However you'd say it out loud.

02

Get your PDF

InvoiceMate reads your message, extracts every detail, calculates VAT if you're registered, and sends back a branded PDF invoice in 30 seconds.

03

Forward & get paid

Forward the PDF to your customer straight from WhatsApp. Include a payment link so they can pay immediately. Done.
